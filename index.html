Welcome to the official site of Hunkoria Archive!


Introduction

The Hunkoria Archive is a comprehensive repository of information, research, and documentation related to the Hunkoria Dimension and its inhabitants. It serves as a central hub for researchers, scientists, and enthusiasts to access valuable insights, discoveries, and records about the enigmatic world of Hunkoria. The archive encompasses a wide range of topics, including the history of the Hunkoria Dimension, its unique flora and fauna, scientific studies conducted by the HEO, and much more. Whether you're a seasoned researcher or simply curious about the mysteries of Hunkoria, this archive is your gateway to unlocking the secrets of this fascinating dimension.

What is HEO?


The Hunkoria-Eila Organization (HEO) is a secretive and highly specialized organization dedicated to the exploration, research, and protection of the Hunkoria Dimension. Founded by Mr. H████ and HDC_██, the HEO operates with a mission to uncover the mysteries of the Hunkoria Universe, study its unique inhabitants, and safeguard humanity from any potential threats that may arise from this enigmatic dimension. The organization is composed of a diverse team of scientists, researchers, field operatives, and support staff who work tirelessly to advance our understanding of the Hunkoria Dimension while ensuring the safety and security of both the public and the scientific community.

    The HEO has 3 main missions:

  1. Research and Exploration: Conducting scientific research and exploration missions to study the nature of the Hunkoria Dimension, its inhabitants (HDCs), and its unique paranormal phenomena.
  2. Protection and Security: Safeguarding humanity from potential threats that may arise from the Hunkoria Dimension, including monitoring the rifts for any signs of danger and responding to incidents as necessary.
  3. Infohazard Management: Managing and mitigating the risks associated with information hazards connected to the existence of the Hunkoria Dimension.
